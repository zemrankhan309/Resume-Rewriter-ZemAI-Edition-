Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Resume Rewriter (ZemAI Edition) is designed with privacy as a core principle. This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. All processing happens locally in your browser.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

This extension does not collect or store:

  • Personal information
  • Names, emails, or contact details
  • Resumes or job descriptions
  • Browsing history
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • IP addresses or device identifiers

2. Your API Key

The Gemini API key you provide is stored locally in your browser using Chrome’s built‑in storage. It is never sent to any server other than Google’s Gemini API endpoint for the purpose of generating rewritten resumes and ATS analysis.

You may delete your API key at any time by clearing the extension’s storage or removing the extension.

3. How Your Data Is Used

When you click “Rewrite Resume” or “Analyze with ATS,” the extension sends your resume and job description directly to Google’s Gemini API. No other servers are involved.

The extension does not store or transmit your resume or job description anywhere else.

4. Local Processing

All UI rendering, ATS scoring display, DOCX generation, and copy‑to‑clipboard actions occur entirely on your device. No data leaves your browser except the request you intentionally send to Gemini.

5. Third‑Party Services

This extension communicates only with:

  • Google Gemini API — for rewriting and ATS analysis

Google’s use of your data is governed by their own privacy policy.

6. No Advertising, No Tracking

This extension contains:

  • No ads
  • No analytics
  • No tracking scripts
  • No cookies

7. Data Retention

The extension does not retain any data. All content remains in your browser until you close the tab or clear your local storage.

8. Your Consent

By using this extension, you consent to the processing of your resume and job description solely for the purpose of generating rewritten content and ATS analysis.
